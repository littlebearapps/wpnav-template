# Content Audit Prompt

Please perform a content audit of my WordPress site.

## Instructions

1. Read `snapshots/site_index.json` to understand my site structure
2. Review individual page snapshots in `snapshots/pages/`
3. Identify issues and improvement opportunities

## Audit Categories

### Content Inventory
- List all pages with title, word count estimate, and last updated date
- Identify pages with very little content (<100 words)
- Find orphaned pages (no internal links)
- Check for missing critical pages (Contact, About, Privacy Policy)

### Content Quality
- Review headlines and introductions
- Check content structure (headings hierarchy)
- Identify pages missing calls-to-action

### SEO Basics
- Check for proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- Identify pages without clear page titles
- Note any duplicate content

## Report Format

Provide findings as a prioritized list:

### Critical Issues (Fix Immediately)
- Issue + recommendation

### High Priority (Fix Soon)
- Issue + recommendation

### Improvement Opportunities
- Suggestion + expected benefit

## Next Steps

After the audit, help me create a plan to address the highest-priority issues.
